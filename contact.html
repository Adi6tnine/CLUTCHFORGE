Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for tournaments?

You can register for tournaments through our events page or by filling out the contact form with "Tournament Registration" as the subject. We'll send you detailed instructions and payment information.

What games do you organize tournaments for?

We currently organize tournaments for BGMI, Free Fire, Valorant, CS:GO, and Call of Duty Mobile. We're constantly expanding to include more popular games based on community demand.

How do I become a campus partner?

Contact us through the form above with "Campus Partnership" as the subject. Include details about your college, student body size, and any previous gaming events you've organized.

Do you provide coaching or training?

Yes! Our club members get access to exclusive training sessions and mentorship from experienced players. We also organize skill development workshops regularly.

How are prizes distributed?

Prizes are distributed within 7 business days after tournament completion. We support multiple payment methods including UPI, bank transfer, and digital wallets.
