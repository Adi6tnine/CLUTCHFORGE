About CLUTCHFORGE

The story behind India's next-generation gaming community

Our Story

CLUTCHFORGE was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a platform where Indian gamers could showcase their skills, connect with like-minded individuals, and transform their passion into purpose.

Founded in 2024 by a group of passionate Gen-Z gamers, CLUTCHFORGE emerged from the recognition that India's gaming community needed a dedicated space that understood their unique culture, aspirations, and potential.

What started as weekend gaming sessions among friends has evolved into a comprehensive ecosystem that supports gamers at every level - from casual players discovering their competitive edge to aspiring professionals seeking their breakthrough moment.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between passion and profession in gaming by providing comprehensive platforms, resources, and opportunities that empower Indian gamers to achieve their full potential.

  • Creating networking opportunities within the industry
